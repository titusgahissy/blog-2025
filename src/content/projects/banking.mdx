A significant portion of people across Africa still lack access to electronic transaction infrastructure. This is a challenge that won't resolve itself without deliberate action.

I've recently secured pre-seed investment to explore several ideas I've been developing for some time. The goal is to create accessible financial tools that serve those who have been overlooked by traditional banking systems.
