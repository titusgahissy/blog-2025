In 2023, my partner and I decided to transform our passion for design and technology into a business, an interior design studio
with a custom-made software to manage projects and clients.

The result is a truly unique tool, applauded by clients because of its ease of use and its ability to manage projects from start to finish (inquiry, design, budget, articles and more).

The next step is to raise funds and make it available to other interior designers studios and agencies.
